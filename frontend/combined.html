About EELU Library Hub

Our Mission

The EELU Library Hub is dedicated to providing comprehensive academic resources to support students and faculty of the Egyptian E-Learning University. We serve both the Business Administration (BA) and Information Technology (IT) faculties with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources.

Inclusive Education

We are committed to supporting all students, including those with special needs. Our platform includes accessibility features such as screen reader support, adjustable text sizes, high contrast modes, and access to braille materials and audio books.
