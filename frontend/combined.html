Our Mission

The EELU Library Hub is dedicated to providing comprehensive academic resources for students and faculty of the Egyptian E-Learning University. We serve the Business Administration (BA) and Information Technology (IT) faculties with books, journals, and digital resources to support learning and research.

Inclusive Education

We are committed to supporting all students, including those with special needs. Our platform offers accessibility features such as screen reader support, adjustable text sizes, high contrast modes, and access to braille materials and audio books.
